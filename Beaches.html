Most Popular Beaches In GUJARAT

  1. Ahmedpur Mandvi Beach:

    2.
      The Ahmedpur Mandvi Beach is a fine and beautiful looking beach located in Ahmedpur Mandvi, Gujarat and near to Diu. The beach is a great place for long walks and swimming. The sand here is firm and white and the place is pretty exotic in appearance. It has great resorts and hotels for accommodation and is the perfect place for holidays, particularly March to July.

    How To Reach:

    By Road: Somnath is the nearest city to Ahmedpur Mandvi Beach which has the connectivity from different places of Gujarat.
    By Rail: If you want come by train, you can come to Veraval railway station. It is about to 6 km from Somnath.
    By Air: Keshod is the nearest airport Somnath with has the regular connectivity from Ahmadabad and Mumbai.

  2. Chorwad Beach:

    3.
      Chorwad is a small fishing village, near Somnath city, Gujarat. This place is known for famous water sports such as boat riding. The cool breeze here will soothe your senses and give you a very pleasant and beautiful feeling. It is also a base point for visiting places such as Porbandar and Somnath.

    How To Reach:

    By Road: Chorwad is about 25 km from Somnath. The Somnath city has the connectivity from different places of Gujarat.
    By Rail: Veraval is the nearest railway station to Somnath. It is about to 6 km from Somnath.
    By Air: Keshod is the nearest airport Somnath with has the regular connectivity from Ahmadabad and Mumbai.

  3. Gopnath Beach:

    4.
      The Gopnath Beach in Gujarat is mesmerizing in appearance and is breathtakingly beautiful. It is scenic and is known for its limestone cliffs and bird life. This is an ideal place to come during summer holidays as this will provide you with all the relaxation you need. To visit this place, you can come by road from Bhavnagar. Bhavnagar has the road, rail and air connectivity from Ahmedabad.

    How To Reach:

    By Road: The nearest main connectivity of Gopnath beach is Bhavnagar and it is about 71 km distance. State transport providing regular services to Bhavnagar from Ahmadabad.
    By Rail: Bhavnagar has the railway connectivity from Ahmadabad.
    By Air: Bhavnagar is connected with Ahmadabad by domestic airlines.

  4. Dwarka Beach:

    5.
      The Dwaraka Beach is the best side for Hindu pilgrimage in Gujarat. It is a very pleasant spot for short holidays in Gujarat. The aquatic life here is mind-blowing as you will find sea turtles, octopus, starfish and urchins on the surface of the water. Spend some good time with your family here.

    How To Reach:

    By Road: Dwaraka has direct bus connectivity from Jamnagar And Ahmadabad. It is about 131 km from Jamnagar and 442 km from Ahmadabad.
    By Rail: It has small railway station connecting to all major stations.
    By Air: Jamnagar airport is the nearest airport.

  5. Nagoa Beach: Diu

    6.
      Nagoa Beach is a very long one which is located in the Nagoa hamlet of the village Bucharwada, Diu. The place is so beautiful and is filled with palm trees which increase its charm and beauty. It is known for its famous water sports such as jet skiing, knee board surfing and swimming. You should also try the pony and camel riding which is always so much fun.

    How To Reach:

    By Road: Nagoa beach is about 7 km away from Diu. There are number of bus services available from various cities of Gujarat.
    By Rail: The Delawada station is the nearest connectivity to Nagoa beach. It is about 9 km.
    By Air: Various domestic airlines connect to Diu from Gujarat.
